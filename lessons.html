Isabella offers private and group art lessons in Beverly Hills, Culver City, Westwood, and Santa Monica, bringing the studio to your home. Group sessions accommodate up to five students. Payment is collected at the time of the lesson. Cash, Venmo, or Zelle accepted.

Teaching Style

Young artists of all levels are welcome. With over five years of teaching experience across various ages and disciplines, Isabella combines her lifelong passion for art with a versatile teaching approach. While her classes are rooted in drawing concepts, these serve only as starting points. Lessons are customized to each students' unique interests, and Isabella encourages exploration through play and experimentation. Students will develop creative problem-solving skills and gain confidence in working with a variety of materials and methods.
